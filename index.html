Meet Maggie

Maggie Germano is a Certified Financial Education Instructor and financial coach for women. She is also a staunch feminist and Michael Jackson enthusiast.

She founded Maggie Germano Financial Coaching with the mission to provide women with the support and information they need to take control of their money and reach their goals.

In her spare time, she likes to go to concerts, garden, brew kombucha, and travel.
