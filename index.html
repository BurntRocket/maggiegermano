Empowering women to heal their money wounds and reach their financial goals

In the United States, we are largely not taught how to manage our money, or even how to make a budget. It is considered taboo to discuss earnings, debt, and financial goals. Women especially are not encouraged or given the tools needed to take control of their financial situation and prepare for their future.

This can lead to financial illiteracy, debt, no savings, and postponement of goals. These things cause many young women to feel anxious, confused, ashamed, and lost when it comes to money. They feel like they will never get out of debt, and they worry that their dreams are out of reach.

My mission is to give women the support and the tools that they need to heal their money wounds, change their money mindset, and achieve their goals and dreams. I will do this through one-on-one financial coaching, Money Circle, webinars, workshops, and more.

If you are ready to take control of your money and your life, let’s work together!
